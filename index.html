What we do

capabilities.

Hive combines AI, DeFi, and compliance into a single, fully on-chain ecosystem on Ritual Chain.

🔐

ZK Identity & Compliance

Groth16-based KYC/KYB verification with on-chain identity. Semi-custodial wallets with dual-auth (primary + hive wallet). Full regulatory compliance without sacrificing privacy.

🧠

AI Decision Engine

HiveBrain orchestrates all AI operations — price feeds via Allora, federated learning via FLock, and autonomous strategy execution. Powered by Ritual LLM precompiles.

⚡

AI Market Maker

Constant-product AMM with AI-driven pricing. Liquidity pools, LP rewards, and automated market making with real-time price oracle integration.

🏛️

DAO Governance

Multi-sig council, proposal system, voting, and treasury management. HiveClearing batch auctions (HCA) for fair token launches. Fully decentralized decision-making.

🔗

EigenLayer AVS

Operator registration, delegation, slashing, and fee distribution. Four operator roles: MarketMaker, InferenceNode, Validator, PriceOracle. Shared security via EigenLayer restaking.

🤖

Autonomous Agents

Queen orchestrator routes tasks to Brain, Strategy, and Drone agents. Automated strategies, point systems, referral rewards, and gamified participation.

Who we are

about us.

Building the future of compliant DeFi

Hive is a fully on-chain AI launchpad built on Ritual Chain (Chain ID 1979). We combine zero-knowledge proofs, artificial intelligence, and decentralized governance to create a compliant, privacy-preserving DeFi ecosystem.

Our architecture features 29 smart contracts, all fully interconnected and tested with 300 passing tests. From ZK-based KYC/KYB to AI-driven market making, every component is designed for security, scalability, and regulatory compliance.

We're building on Ritual Chain's native precompiles — LLM inference, HTTP requests, DKMS key management, ECIES encryption, and Passkey authentication — to deliver capabilities that traditional EVM chains simply cannot match.
