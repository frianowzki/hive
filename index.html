Who we are

About us

Building the future of compliant DeFi

Hive is a fully on-chain AI launchpad built on Ritual Chain (Chain ID 1979). We combine zero-knowledge proofs, artificial intelligence, and decentralized governance to create a compliant, privacy-preserving DeFi ecosystem.

Our architecture features 29 smart contracts, all fully interconnected and tested with 300 passing tests. From ZK-based KYC/KYB to AI-driven market making, every component is designed for security, scalability, and compliance.

We leverage Ritual Chain's native precompiles — LLM inference, HTTP requests, DKMS key management, ECIES encryption, and Passkey authentication — to deliver capabilities that traditional EVM chains cannot match.
