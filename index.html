Features

ZK Identity & Compliance

Groth16-based KYC/KYB verification with on-chain identity. Semi-custodial wallets with dual-auth. Full regulatory compliance without exposing personal data.

AI Decision Engine

HiveBrain orchestrates AI operations — price feeds via Allora, federated learning via FLock, autonomous strategies. Think → Plan → Act pipeline with confidence threshold.

AI Market Maker

Constant-product AMM with AI-driven pricing. Liquidity pools, LP rewards, real-time oracle integration. On-chain AI determines optimal token launch price.

DAO Governance

Multi-sig council, proposal system, voting, treasury management. HiveClearing batch auctions for fair token launches with AI-driven price discovery.

Security & Privacy

Ritual native precompiles — DKMS key management, ECIES encryption, Passkey authentication. TEE-bound keys never leave the enclave. Privacy-preserving by design.

Autonomous Agents

Queen orchestrator routes tasks to Brain, Strategy, and Drone agents. Automated trading strategies, on-chain points, 4-tier referral engine.

About us

Building the future of compliant DeFi

Hive is a fully on-chain AI launchpad built on Ritual Testnet (Chain ID 1979). We combine zero-knowledge proofs, artificial intelligence, and decentralized governance to create a compliant, privacy-preserving DeFi ecosystem.

Our architecture features 29 smart contracts, all fully interconnected and tested with 300 passing tests. Every component is designed for security, scalability, and compliance.

We leverage Ritual Testnet's native precompiles — LLM inference, HTTP requests, DKMS key management, ECIES encryption, and Passkey authentication.
